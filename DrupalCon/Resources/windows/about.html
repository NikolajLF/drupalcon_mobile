About DrupalCon Chicago

DrupalCon is an international event that brings together the people who use, develop, design, and support the Drupal platform. DrupalCon Chicago will be held March 7-10, 2011, at the Chicago Sheraton Hotel and Towers.

The conference feature dozens of curated sessions and panels from some of the most influential people and brightest minds within the Drupal community and beyond, as well as countless opportunities for networking, sprints, informal conversations, and more.

About the conference

Core Conversations

Core Conversations are targeted at those actively engaged in helping shape the future of Drupal through conversations about software architecture, development process, user experience, and more. Sponsored by NYSE.

Day Stage

The DrupalCon Day Stage, located in the Exhibit Hall, is the venue for exclusive content such as lightning talks, case studies, sponsor demonstrations, and other unique and entertaining programming. Sponsored by Duo Consulting.

Drupal Means Business

Drupal Means Business is an Exhibit Hall event that will be held on Thursday, March 10. Day Stage programming for this event will include case studies and other content be targeted at evaluators and other decision-makers.

About this application

The DrupalCon mobile application lets you view session schedules, descriptions, maps, and presenter bios from the palm of your hand, as well as live updates from the official DrupalCon Twitter feed. The DrupalCon mobile application for iPhone and Android was developed by Larry Garfield, Jen Simmons, and Pat Teglia of Palantir.net using the Appcelerator Titanium mobile framework.
